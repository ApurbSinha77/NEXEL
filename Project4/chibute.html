Career Highlights

Famous for playing every Instagram user who don't leave their phone screen 24/7. Ryan Gosling is a Canadian actor known for his versatile and acclaimed career. He began as a child actor on Disney's "The Mickey Mouse Club" and transitioned into film, gaining recognition for roles in "The Notebook" and "Half Nelson."
